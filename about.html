About TLC

TLC is devoted to everyday pampering, relaxation and peace. We are the destination for anyone who wants to de-stress and unwind.

Our Story

TLC was founded by four friends, Alex, Lucy, Zahrah and Tina. The idea came about during university when the girls discovered that there is limited information online about spas and relaxation. As a result TLC aims to:

  1. Inspire and give tips on relaxation methods
  2. Give people more access and information about spas
  3. Promote self-care and self-love rituals
